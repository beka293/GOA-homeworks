USA

The United States of America (USA), often called America, is a vast federal republic spanning North America. It is the world's third-largest country by population and land area, known for its diverse landscapes and global influence

Population and Culture

The population exceeds 330 million, making it a "nation of immigrants" from Europe, Africa, Asia, and Latin America. English is the main language, but Spanish is widely spoken. Americans celebrate diverse holidays like Thanksgiving and Independence Day on July 4th.
